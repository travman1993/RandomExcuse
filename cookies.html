Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how Caught-Slippin uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your experience more convenient and the site more useful.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

Purpose: Required for the website to function properly.

Examples:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Load balancing

Can be disabled: No (site won't work without them)

2. Functional Cookies

Purpose: Remember your preferences and choices.

Examples:

  • Last selected category
  • Tone preference (serious/funny)
  • Display preferences

Can be disabled: Yes (through browser settings)

3. Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Help us understand how visitors use our site.

What we track:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Button clicks and interactions
  • Browser and device information

Provider: Google Analytics

Can be disabled: Yes (through browser settings or opt-out tools)

4. Advertising Cookies

Purpose: Deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance.

Provider: Google AdSense and partners

Can be disabled: Yes (through ad settings or browser settings)

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to save:

  • Your saved replies
  • Your saved tips
  • Recent activity

Important: This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand site usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information anonymously.

  • Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Google AdSense

We display ads through Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies to personalize ads based on your visits to this and other sites.

  • Learn more: How Google uses cookies in advertising

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve functionality: Remember your preferences
  • Enhance security: Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Analyze performance: Understand how users interact with the site
  • Deliver ads: Show relevant advertisements
  • Measure effectiveness: Track which features are most useful

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Mobile Devices

  • iOS: Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies
  • Android: Settings → Site settings → Cookies

Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

  • Some features may not work properly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • You may see less relevant ads
  • We won't be able to track site improvements

Note: Essential cookies cannot be disabled, or the site won't function.

Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

Deleted when you close your browser.

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. Our persistent cookies typically last:

  • Functional cookies: Up to 1 year
  • Analytics cookies: Up to 2 years
  • Advertising cookies: Varies by provider (typically 1-2 years)

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals. We do not currently respond to DNT signals, but we minimize data collection as described in this policy.
