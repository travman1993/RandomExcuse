Response Time

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please mark your email subject with "[URGENT]".

Report Inappropriate Content

If you encounter a generated response or tip that seems harmful, inappropriate, or encourages dangerous behavior, please let us know immediately. We take content quality seriously and review all reports.

When reporting content, please include:

  • The category where you found it
  • The tone (serious or funny)
  • A brief description of the issue
  • A screenshot if possible
